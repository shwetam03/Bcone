ASSUMPTIONS

Timesheet submitted by Tuesday 12:00 pm IST is considered "In time"
Timesheet submitted beyond Tuesday 12:00 pm IST is considered "Delayed"
Consultants on bench are considered as non-billable. Hence they should book time as non-billable
Consultants worked on pre-sales/training/recruitment should be booking time as non-billable
A consultant may be deployed on project for on-job learning. In such circumstances you are required to book time in shadow consulting phase of bench project as non-billable
Personal time off: When a consultant avails leave, appropriate time-type specific to leave such as “Casual leave” should be selected. “Billable & non-billable” time types should be used only when consultant attends office.
